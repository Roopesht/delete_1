## What Success Looks Like

### How It Feels (Visitor & Member Experience)

_Success is when people feel the following — not just when numbers go up._

**Anonymous visitor**
- "This is exactly what I've been looking for" — the site answers the quest for structured trading education, not tips
- "This person actually knows what they're talking about" — trainer credibility lands within the first scroll
- "This is different from every other trading service I've seen" — no signals, no noise, no false promises
- "I feel safe registering here" — clear disclaimer, no aggressive sales, no overwhelming jargon

**Newly registered user**
- "I know what happens next" — confirmation page sets clear expectations (WhatsApp, Telegram, next steps)
- "I made the right decision" — the welcome experience feels structured and professional, not spammy

**Telegram member**
- "I'm actually learning to think, not just follow" — setups are educational, not instructions to execute blindly
- "This community is worth staying in" — engagement is meaningful, not noise
- "I can see myself getting better" — progress feels tangible over weeks

**Enrolled student (TRC / F&O)**
- "I'm not left alone after paying" — support is present and responsive
- "This was worth the money" — outcome justifies the investment
- "I know what to do next" — clear path from TRC to F&O, no confusion about progression

---

### Growth
- Consistent growth in site visits
- Consistent growth in registrations
- Increasing Telegram engagement
- Stable or improving conversion rates

### Community
- Telegram feels active: members ask questions, respond to setups, share observations
- Low opt-out rate — members stay because the content is valuable
- Referrals happening organically — existing members bring in new registrations

### Learning & Outcomes
- Members report more structured, independent trading decisions
- A portion of TRC graduates progress to F&O — signals real learning, not just curiosity

### Website Attractiveness & Usability
- Bounce rate < 60% (visitors explore beyond the landing page)
- Mobile experience is smooth — the primary audience (40+, homemakers, retirees) is on mobile
- Page load < 3 seconds on mobile
- Registration form is simple enough to complete without help

### Trust & Credibility
- Trainer credibility understood quickly from the home page (25+ years, institutions)
- New testimonials collected from TRC / F&O graduates each quarter
- No negative sentiment about misleading promises
- Disclaimer and "no tips / no advice" positioning consistently visible

### Revenue
- Revenue is predictable: consistent enrollments each week, not spiky
- F&O revenue grows as TRC graduates naturally progress
